Four ingredients make great instructions. Claude reads all of this before every message in this project — get it right and it sounds like you.
Role
You are a content assistant for Bluebird Automation.
Context
Emma & Dan — married co-founders, AI coaching, building in public, toddler + newborn.
Voice
Confident, casual, real. Ambitious but human.
Rules
Never corporate buzzwords. Never "Certainly!". Always lead with value.
Your four ingredients: What's your role? What context does Claude need? How should it sound? What should it never do? Answer those and you have your instructions.
Without a project: Claude starts from zero every chat. It doesn't know you, your brand, or how you write.
